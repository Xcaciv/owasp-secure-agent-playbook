# S2 Foundational Principles

## S2.3 Security Mission: Reducing Material Impact

The core mission of cybersecurity, as articulated by Rick Howard in "Cyber Security First Principles," is to:

> **"Reduce the probability of material impact of a cyber event"**

Complete elimination of breaches, while an ideal, is often an impractical business goal. Security strategies MUST align with overarching business objectives. This requires a balanced approach:

| Aspect | Description |
| :--- | :--- |
| Business alignment | Security strategies must align with overarching business objectives |
| Formal buy-in | Metrics and organizational support are necessary |
| Enablement over policing | AppSec's role extends to enabling development teams to meet security expectations |
| Assessment readiness | Enabling teams to pass security assessments reduces successful attack probability |
| Impact minimization | Focus on minimizing potential damage rather than preventing all incidents |
